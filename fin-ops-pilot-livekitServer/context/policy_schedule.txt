**Claims Process**  
1. **Cashless Claim Process (for Accidental Hospitalization Cover):**  
   - Notice must be given within 24 hours of hospitalization in emergencies.  
   - Present Health Card/E-Cards and ID Proof to the hospital, obtain a Pre-Authorization Form, and submit it to the hospital counter.  
   - Ensure treatment occurs within 15 days of Pre-Authorization approval or by the policy expiry date.  
   - The insurer reserves the right to modify the list of Network Providers for the Cashless Facility.  

2. **Reimbursement Claim Process:**  
   - Intimation to the TPA must occur 48 hours before admission.  
   - Submit all original hospitalization documents within 30 days of discharge.  
   - Claims will be settled or repudiated within 30 days of receiving the last necessary document, with interest paid at a rate of 2% above the bank rate for delays.  
   - In the event of the policyholder's death, the claim amount will be reimbursed to the named nominee or legal representative with a valid succession certificate.
