**Claims Related to Accidental Injury or Critical Illness:**
1. **Cashless Claim Process:**
   - Notice must be given within 24 hours of hospitalization in emergencies.
   - Procedure includes sharing the Health Card/E-Cards and ID Proof with the hospital, submitting a Pre-Authorization Form, and ensuring the hospital sends this form to the Service Provider/Third Party Administrator (TPA).
   - Treatment must occur within 15 days of Pre-Authorization approval or by the policy expiry date, whichever is earlier.

2. **Reimbursement Claim Process:**
   - Intimate the Service Provider/TPA 48 hours prior to admission.
   - Submit all original hospitalization documents within 30 days from the date of discharge.
   - Claims will be settled or repudiated within 30 days of receiving the last necessary document. If payment is delayed beyond 30 days, interest will be paid at a rate of 2% above the bank rate at the start of the financial year.
